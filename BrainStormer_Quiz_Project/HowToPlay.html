How to play

  • 1.) Choose your topic of interest
  • 2.) Select your answer by clicking on the circle
  • 3.) If you get the answer right it will go green
  • 4.) If you get the answer wrong it will go red
  • 5.) Score is shown on the bottom left
  • 6.) Click 'Get Results' to find out your score

    • Good luck!!
